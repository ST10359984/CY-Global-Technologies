Our Story

CY Global Technologies CC, established in 2007, is an IT company offering a wide range of services. We specialize in sales of new, demo, and refurbished laptops, PC components and equipment, software and hardware support, PC and laptop repairs, technical support (after-sales, remote, and on-site), custom PC builds, network setup, data recovery, and outsourced web development and design. Our focus is on providing exceptional IT support and service at competitive rates, with a commitment to resolving computer problems quickly. We offer nationwide services, including delivery of purchased equipment and courier services for faulty units.
